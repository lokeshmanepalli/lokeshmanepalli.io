About Me

I am an undergraduate researcher in the Honors Through Research (HTR) Program at Koneru Lakshmaiah Education Foundation (KLEF), specializing in Artificial Intelligence for Computational Intelligence.

My research philosophy is driven by a systems-level approach: I do not just train algorithms; I build end-to-end architectures that solve complex, real-world problems. Currently, my work spans two critical domains: Human-Computer Interaction (Mental Health) and the Water-Energy Nexus (Renewable Energy).

I am currently working under the mentorship of Dr. S. Shanmugan and am preparing to join the International Advanced Research Centre for Powder Metallurgy and New Materials (ARCI) as a Summer Research Intern. After completing my B.Tech, I intend to pursue a Fast-Track PhD in Germany to continue my work on AI-driven sustainable infrastructures.

Featured Research

MITRA: A Deep Learning–Based Emotion-Aware Conversational AI

Featured Work

MITRA is a privacy-first, deep learning–based conversational AI system designed for student mental health assessment and monitoring in educational environments. The architecture combines BiLSTM and CNN models to identify emotional distress from informal text with 94.7% accuracy.

This work was presented at IIT Patna Research Conference ICADCA (Dec 2025) and reflects my broader research vision of building intelligent, human-centered systems that are both technically strong and ethically grounded.

Independent Research • Status: Completed

Focused on cybersecurity and data ethics in conversational agents. This study analyzed over 2,300 data breaches across 15 chatbot platforms from 2004 to 2021 and found that 62% of platforms lacked strong encryption while 14% showed vulnerability to data poisoning.
