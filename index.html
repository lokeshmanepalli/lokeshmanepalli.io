About Me

I am an undergraduate researcher in the Honors Through Research (HTR) Program at Koneru Lakshmaiah Education Foundation (KLEF), specializing in Artificial Intelligence for Computational Intelligence.

My research philosophy is driven by a systems-level approach: I do not just train algorithms; I build end-to-end architectures that solve complex, real-world problems. Currently, my work spans two critical domains: Human-Computer Interaction (Mental Health) and the Water-Energy Nexus (Renewable Energy).

I am currently working under the mentorship of Dr. S. Shanmugan and am preparing to join the International Advanced Research Centre for Powder Metallurgy and New Materials (ARCI) as a Summer Research Intern. After completing my B.Tech, I intend to pursue a Fast-Track PhD in Germany to continue my work on AI-driven sustainable infrastructures.

Featured Research

MITRA: A Deep Learning–Based Emotion-Aware Conversational AI

Featured Work

MITRA is a privacy-first, deep learning–based conversational AI system designed for student mental health assessment and monitoring in educational environments. The architecture combines BiLSTM and CNN models to identify emotional distress from informal text with 94.7% accuracy.

This work was presented at IIT Patna Research Conference ICADCA (Dec 2025) and reflects my broader research vision of building intelligent, human-centered systems that are both technically strong and ethically grounded.

Research Papers & Publications

Data and Privacy Risks in AI Chatbots

Independent Research • Status: Completed

Focused on cybersecurity and data ethics in conversational agents. This study analyzed over 2,300 data breaches across 15 chatbot platforms from 2004 to 2021 and found that 62% of platforms lacked strong encryption while 14% showed vulnerability to data poisoning.

This served as my foundational research project and directly informed the privacy, security, and data-anonymization framework later used in the MITRA chatbot architecture.

Cybersecurity AI Ethics Chatbots

MITRA: A Deep Learning–Based Emotion-Aware Conversational AI

First Author • Status: Presented at IIT Patna Research Conference ICADCA (Dec 2025) • Under Review at International Journal of Artificial Intelligence in Education (Springer)

Focused on mental health assessment and monitoring in educational environments. I developed a BiLSTM-CNN hybrid deep learning architecture that achieved 94.7% accuracy in detecting student emotional distress from informal text.

The system was built on a privacy-first design, utilizing end-to-end encryption to protect sensitive student data.

Deep Learning Mental Health AI NLP

AI-Based Solar Desalination for Green Hydrogen Production

Co-Authored with Dr. S. Shanmugan • Status: Under Review – Renewable and Sustainable Energy Reviews (Elsevier)

A comprehensive 70+ page architectural review bridging the Water-Energy-Hydrogen nexus. This work proposes a conceptual digital twin framework and an AI-driven adaptive control system for renewable desalination and green hydrogen generation.

The architecture integrates smart photothermal materials for continuous and optimized hydrogen production.

Renewable Energy Hydrogen Solar Desalination

Interpretable Text Message Spam Detection via a Novel Fuzzy–Bidirectional XGBoost Architecture

First Author • Status: Manuscript Complete – Ready for Submission

Focused on explainable AI, natural language processing, and cybersecurity. I engineered a tri-level feature representation system combining TF-IDF, BiLSTM-based semantic features, and fuzzy linguistic modeling.

Integrated with an XGBoost classifier and SHAP analysis, the model achieved 98.24% accuracy and 93.19% F1-score while maintaining strong human-readability and transparency.
